From the soil to the soul, groundnuts enrich every bite.

"Groundnuts have a versatile culinary use; they can be eaten raw, roasted, boiled, or used as an ingredient in various dishes. They are widely used in the production of peanut butter, oil, flour, and are a common ingredient in many Asian, African, and Southern American cuisines, featuring in sauces, stews, and marinades. Additionally, groundnuts are often processed into snacks, from simple salted or spiced nuts to more complex sweets and desserts."
